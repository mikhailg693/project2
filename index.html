Mission

Our mission is to bring alive the power of curiosity and passion in each and every student and to help maximize their academic potential. Manhattan Hunter Science High School is a science-focused college-preparatory high school designed to give students the tools they need to achieve academic success. Hunter College is an integral part of Manhattan Hunter Science High School, from collaborating with faculty and enrolling students in college-level courses on its own Upper East Side campus, to providing a full time 12th grade college experience where students develop important early college skills, including academic research and writing, effective study habits, and time management.

Admissions

We look for students with a strong record of attendance, grades of 80 and above, and levels 2-4 on NY State Examinations. We receive thousands of applications and only have space for approximately 100 students, it is very important to rank us high on your HS preference sheet. The higher the rank, the more likely a match. Students who wish to attend Manhattan/Hunter Science High School must submit the following materials by December 3, 2018. Academic Reports

Each year the New York Times reports on the most competitive high schools in New York City public schools, excluding the specialized high schools. In 2017, Manhattan Hunter Science High School was ranked number one as the most competitive high school in NYC.

New York Times

In 2015, Manhattan/Hunter Science was ranked #14 of the New York Post's top 50 high schools and placed again in the 2013 report. In 2010, Manhattan/Hunter Science was ranked #16 of the New York Post's top 50 high schools.

New York Post

MHSHS has consistently received A's on the NYC Department of Education Progress Report since its inception. The Progress Report aims to evaluate our school's performance in three key areas: student progress, student performance, and learning environment.
